Upload Products

The Supplier can upload a product into the blockchin.The hash of the ZIP file is calculated and stored on the Blockchain together with the date of submission for later verification.

Verify Product Submission

The hash provided should match the one stored in the Blockchain (created during product submission). If the stored product has been modified after submission, the hash will not match with the one stored on the Blockchain.
