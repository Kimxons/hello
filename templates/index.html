What We Do..

We are a not-for-profit organization with a mission to improve transparency, trust & traceability of maize in the Agricultural supply chain. We provide the Right Tools for the Participants in the Maize Supply chain

Information

Most farmers are operating at just 40% of their potential capacity access to information, farmers can now plan and harvest more effectively and gain access to market.

Immutable Data

The Distributed Ledger Technology solution provides immutable information providing trust and transparency throughout the supply chain. Each block of information proceed using a hash value

Traceability

Lack of traceability means tracking and recalling specific contaminated products throughout the supply chain impossible. With the DLT solution, each item can be traced from seed to consumer.

Core Features

Software as a Service (SaaS)

Our platform allows supply chain participants to achieve the following services:

  • Farmers connect with key actors in the supply chain

  • Distributors can carry out their activities without interruption by greedy intermediries

  • Retailers can communicate directly with the farmers and everyone in the supply chain.

  • Consumers to purchase maize across the supply chain.

  • Improved agricultural productivity.

  • Smallholder economic development.

  • Increased profit through value addition.
